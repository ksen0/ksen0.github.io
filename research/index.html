Current Work

Radical Imperfection

Through personal practice and collaborative research, I'm currently studying self-tracking tools and methods. Recently, I have been teaching practice-based courses to explore, through critical discussion and applied practice, the following questions:

  • Feb-Mar 2020: Radical Impefection in Time Tracking: What makes the experience of observing our creative selves through data surprising, intriguing, and even healing?
  • Nov-Dec 2020: Radical Impefection in Self Tracking: How can we create sustainable, transformative, systematic self-reflection practices?
