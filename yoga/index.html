Hello!

My name is Kit Kuksenok (they/them) and I teach vinyasa yoga classes, which bind movement with breath as we flow through a sequence of asana, or poses. You don't need any specific prior experience or ability, just curiousity.

My yoga journey timeline:

  • (ongoing, until Dec. 2021) 300H Advanced Vinyasa and Restorative Practices YTT @ KALAA Yoga Berlin
  • 2019-2020: 200H Vinyasa Yoga Teacher Training
    @ KALAA Yoga Berlin
  • 2011/2012: I first encountered yoga in Seattle, and it became an essential part of working with chronic pain associated with wrist injury. This experience continues to inform my personal yoga practice, and my teaching style; even in more challenging sequences, I try to reduce excess stress on shoulders/wrists, while building strength, balance, and mobility in these areas (more info below...)

Wrist and Shoulder Care

All classes marked withüëêaim to minimize excess stress on shoulders/wrists, the sequences will focus on developing balance, control, and strength in the shoulders through:

(1) warming up the shoulders, neck, and wrists
(2) not placing entire body weight onto wrists
(3) using forearm variations for arm balances
