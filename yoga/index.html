Hello!

My name is Kit Kuksenok, and I am a transgender non-binary person (my pronouns are they/them). I teach Vinyasa Yoga, which focuses on binding movement with breath as we flow through a sequence of Asana, or poses. You don't need any specific prior experience or ability, just curiousity. The body is not a tool for finding a particular posture; the movement is a tool to explore the body.

For me, the cultivation of discipline and awareness is both the purpose and the method of Yoga Asana practice. I initially discovered Yoga Asana in 2011, and completed my 200H Teacher Training in 2019-2020 in KALAA Yoga Berlin.

The classes are free and small. I am at the beginning of my teaching journey, and I am grateful for the opportunity to practice teching. Teaching online from my home allows me to keep operating costs very low (around 30eur/mo for online tools), and your support is appreciated but not required. If you are able to pay 3-10EUR for a yoga class, consider donating that amount to a charitable organisation of your choice.
